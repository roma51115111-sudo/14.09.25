Our capabilities

  • Volume and 3D depth

    The wall is no longer flat: 3D printing creates the illusion of tangible depth and relief, bringing the mural to life.

  • High quality and detail

    High-resolution printing captures the finest details and hues with the precision you envision.

  • High speed

    Automated printing applies the image to the wall in a matter of hours, providing an almost instantaneous transformation of the space.

  • Individuality

    Each mural is created to suit the interior and style - its subject, texture and colors are individual and emphasize the character of the space.

  • Artistic lighting

    Frescoes reveal themselves differently in natural and artificial light - backlighting creates atmosphere and emphasizes volume.

  • Eco-friendliness

    The durable eco-friendly paints are safe for health and do not fade over time.

  • Interactive

    Support for AR and 3D visualization opens up new levels of engagement and digital interaction.
